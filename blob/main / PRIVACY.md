# Privacy Policy for Agent Blue

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

We believe in keeping things simple and safe. This Privacy Policy explains how the **Agent Blue** browser extension handles your data. 

**The Short Version: We do not collect, store, or sell any of your personal data.**

Here are the details:

## 1. What data do we collect?
**None.** 
Agent Blue is a tool that runs entirely on your own computer. The developer (Muhammad Shahzeb Malik) has no access to your data, your browsing history, your settings, or the tasks you automate. We do not have servers that collect your information.

## 2. Where is your data saved?
**On your device.**
Any settings you save (like API keys or custom instructions) are stored securely on your own computer using your browser's local storage. This information never leaves your device unless you actively use it to run an automation.

## 3. How does the AI work with your data?
Agent Blue uses third-party AI services (like Gemini, GPT-4o, or Groq) to figure out how to automate the webpage you are on. 
* When you type a command to automate a task, the extension sends your command and parts of the current webpage's structure to the AI provider you have selected.
* This is required so the AI knows what buttons are on the screen and how to complete the task for you.
* The data sent to the AI provider is handled according to their own privacy policies (for example, Google's policy for Gemini, or OpenAI's policy for GPT-4o). 

## 4. Why do we need browser permissions?
To automate tasks for you, Agent Blue needs the following standard browser permissions:
* **"Read and change all your data on all websites" (host permissions, activeTab, scripting):** We need this so the extension can "see" the page you are on and perform clicks or type text for you when you ask it to.
* **"Tabs":** So the extension knows which tab it needs to automate.
* **"Storage":** So you can save your settings locally on your computer.
* **"Alarms":** So the extension can handle timed or scheduled tasks in the background.

## 5. Changes to this policy
If we ever change how the extension works in a way that affects your privacy, we will update this document. However, our core promise—that we do not collect or sell your data—will not change.
